## 👋 About Me

I'm a **Frontend Developer** passionate about crafting digital experiences that are as beautiful as they are functional. I specialize in translating designs into clean, production-quality code with a focus on **performance**, **responsiveness**, and **accessibility**.

What sets me apart is my attention to the small details — the micro-interactions, smooth transitions, and consistent spacing — that elevate a good interface into a great one. **Every pixel matters.**

- 🔭 Currently building polished, accessible web apps
- 🌱 Continuously exploring design systems & component architecture
- 💡 Passionate about open-source & modern web development
- 📬 Reach me at **muntazirmahdi.dev@gmail.com**
